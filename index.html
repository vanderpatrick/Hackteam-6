About Us

Explore Our World

Welcome to Out and About, an interactive world map we have created to educate, enlighten, and celebrate the diverse experiences of queer individuals and the movements that have propelled them forward. Here, we delve into the rich tapestry of struggles, triumphs, and milestones that have shaped the LGBTQ+ community throughout history.

By exploring our interactive world map, you'll embark on a captivating journey through time, discovering the remarkable stories that have unfolded across continents and cultures. From ancient civilizations that revered same-sex relationships to modern-day milestones in the fight for LGBTQ+ rights, our website brings together a vast collection of historical events, social movements, and influential figures. ... Join us on this transformative journey through time and space, as we celebrate the triumphs, acknowledge the struggles, and honor the courageous individuals who have paved the way for a more inclusive world. Together, let us create a global community that embraces and uplifts the diverse tapestry of human identities and experiences.
